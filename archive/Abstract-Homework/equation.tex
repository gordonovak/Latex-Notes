    \subsubsection*{Two-Particle System}
    $\hat H=-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m_1}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x_1^2}+V(x_1)$\\
    .$\quad\quad-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m_2}\frac{\partial^2}{\partial x_2^2}+V(x_2)$\en
    Single particle:\n
    $-\frac{\hbar^2}{2m_1}\frac{d^2\psi}{dx_1^2}+V(x_1)\psi = E_1\psi$\en
    Exchange Operator:\n
    $P_{12}\psi_{1}(x_1)\psi_{2}(x_2)= \psi_{1}(x_2)\psi_{2}(x_1)$\n
    $P_{12}\Psi(1,2)=\lambda \Psi(1,2)$.\n
    $\lambda = e^{i\delta},\;\lambda^2=1$.
